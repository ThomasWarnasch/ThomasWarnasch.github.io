About The Primary Archive

We are trying to unify audio drama into a single cohesive platform! Our primary goal is to acumulate audio drama content and organize it into an easy to access platform. The other priorities of The Primary Archive are to provide a streamlined experience with as few resources used. Did you know that our average page size is 15x smaller than your average podcast's website? It also allows our simplistic design to prosper on mobile devices with spotty connections and old machines with slow processors!
